Features

Easy Sign-up

You can always return to the homepage by clicking Alumni Federation logo at any time

  • If you prefer to sign up using your email address, click on "Manual Sign In." Then, click "Create Account" and fill out the registration form
  • In order to protect the privacy of our alumni, after you have created your account the Alumni Federation will verify your identity against our records before granting you access to the site

Update Your Information Online

Update your Alumni DIRECTORY information by clicking on the "My Profile" button on the homepage (once you are signed in). Apart from basic info - such as education, employment, accomplishments - your personal details will only be viewable to the Alumni Federation staff and you

Update your Alumni DIRECTORY information by clicking on the "My Profile" button on the homepage (once you are signed in). Apart from basic info - such as education, employment, accomplishments - your personal details will only be viewable to the Alumni Federation staff and you. You can update information about your social media profiles like Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, etc. You also have the opportunity to list your skills and interests.

Contact Other Alumni

To send a message to an alumnus, select their profile and click "Send Message."

  • Messages will be delivered to the email address of the alumnus regardless of whether they are active on the website or not.
  • If you notice an "Invite to signup" button underneath someone's profile picture, feel free to click it to invite them to join the website.
  • All the contack details are available in their profile

Get Daily new events news,alumni posts and more

Discover alumni events right on the homepage in the "Upcoming Events" section. Selecting an event provides event information and the ability to register online. You may also go find "Events" in navigation bar

Our Mission

The mission of the Connecticut College Alumni Association is to lead alumni in fostering strong connections with each other and Connecticut College as the College assumes its place at the forefront of liberal arts education.

Our Plan

Recognize that alumni offer a great diversity of talents, skills and abilities, which should be acknowledged and engaged to benefit the College, the Association and one another. Provide programs and opportunities that connect alumni to each other and to the College.

Our Vision

To promote a goodwill and sense of pride to both alumni and students. To help and support alumnus develop and spread a philanthropic and entrepreneurial mindset among students. To advice and conduct activities that shall motivate and upgrade student skill sets
